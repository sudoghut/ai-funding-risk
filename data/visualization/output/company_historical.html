2. OCF Breakdown - What Generates Cash Flow?

OCF (Operating Cash Flow) = Net Income + Non-Cash Adjustments + Working Capital Changes

OCF Component Definitions:
  • Net Income ‚Äî Profit after all expenses and taxes (cash basis starting point)
  • D&A (Depreciation & Amortization) ‚Äî Non-cash expense added back; spreads asset costs over time
  • Stock Comp ‚Äî Stock-based compensation; non-cash expense added back
  • Working Capital ‚Äî Changes in receivables, payables, inventory (negative = cash used)
  • Deferred Tax ‚Äî Difference between tax expense and actual tax paid
  • Other ‚Äî Other non-cash adjustments and reconciling items
